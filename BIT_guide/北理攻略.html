How to recharge the campus card?

  1. i北理 > Campus Services（校园服务） > Campus Card Services（校园卡）
  2. Enter the Campus Card Services（校园卡） interface
  3. Select Campus Card Recharge（校园卡充值）
    Important Instructions:
    • Service hours: 05:00-23:00
    • Recharge limit: No more than 200 RMB per transaction
    • Account balance limit: No more than 1000 RMB in your account
  4. Enter the recharge amount(充值金额) > Recharge（充值） > Choose payment method (Alipay/WeChat Pay)

How to recharge campus internet? ⭐

  1. i北理 > Campus Services（校园服务） > Internet Recharge（校园网充值）
  2. Enter the Internet Services（校园网充值） interface
  3. Select Wallet Recharge（钱包充值） (if your e-wallet already has money, skip to step 5), makesure the Recharge Object(充值对象) is EWallet(电子钱包)
  4. Enter the payment amount > Confirm payment > Go back to the previous page
  5. Change the Recharge Object(充值对象) to the internet package(学生-10元含300GB不限速流量)
  6. Enter 10 in the Payment Amount（缴费金额） > Confirm payment

PS: The internet package provide 300GB of unlimited-speed data per month for 10 RMB.

How to connect the campus internet?

Tips: Username is your student ID, and the password is your unified authentication password.

How to connect to BIT-Web on PC/mobile

  1. Open your PC/mobile Wi-Fi settings and select BIT-Web.
  2. It will automatically redirect to a login page, or you can enter 10.0.0.55 in your browser.
  3. Enter your username and password > Log in.
    Once logged in, you can view your data usage and time at 10.0.0.55.

How to connect to BIT-Mobile on PC

  1. Open your PC's Wi-Fi settings and select BIT-Mobile.
  2. Enter your username and password > Log in.

How to connect to BIT-Mobile on mobile

  1. Open your mobile's Wi-Fi settings and select BIT-Mobile.
  2. -> EAP Method: PEAP
  3. -> Phase 2 Authentication: MSCHAPV2
  4. -> CA Certificate: Don't verify
  5. -> Identity: (Your student ID)
  6. -> Anonymous Identity: (Leave blank)
  7. -> Password: (Internet password)
  8. Connect.

More details can be found here

How to pay for electricity online?

  1. i北理 > Campus Services(校园服务) > Logistics Services（后勤服务）> Electricity bill top-up(电费充值)
  2. Click on Room Binding（房间绑定） (if already bound, skip to step 4)
  3. Select "Liangxiang Campus(良乡校区) - International Student Dorm（留学生公寓） - Room Number" > Bind（绑定）.
  4. After binding, click Recharge Now（立即充值） to recharge.

How to Book a Campus Bus?

  1. i北理 > Campus Services（校园服务） > Campus Bus Service(班车服务)

  2. Click "Book Now（立即购票）"

  3. After checking the bus schedule, select your seat

  4. After purchasing the ticket, check your order in My Orders(我的订单)

  5. When boarding, open the order information and scan the QR code to validate your ticket and board the bus

    Note: Students can only purchase tickets one hour before departure!
    Ticket Price: 6 RMB

Rainbow Bus 彩虹班车

To book a Rainbow Bus, find the WeChat public account: 彩虹巴士H2O, verify your identity, and purchase tickets. Reservations can be made up to two weeks in advance.

Where is the Bus Stop?

The three campuses all have bus stops: one next to the tank in the South Campus, one in front of the School Hospital in the North Campus, and one in front of Wen Cui Building A in the East Campus. The school bus departs and arrives in the order of East Campus, North Campus, and South Campus bus stops.

  1. East Campus bus stop

  2. North Campus bus stop

  3. South Campus bus stop

How to View the Class Schedule?

  1. i北理 > Campus Services（校园服务） > My Class Schedule（我的课表）

  2. Check your class schedule and the class timetable to ensure you attend classes on time.

Campus Card Guide

The campus card can be used for:

  • Meals in the canteen
  • Bathroom water
  • ID for exams
  • Library entry and book borrowing
  • Campus access (for security checks)

What to Do If You Lose Your Campus Card? ⭐

If you lose your campus card, you can apply for a new one at the Student Service Center（学生服务中心） (on the left side of the first floor).

A replacement card costs 20 RMB, so make sure to keep your card safe!

Library

The university has two libraries: Xuteli Library 徐特立图书馆 (Liangxiang Campus) and Zhongguancun Library 中关村馆 (Zhongguancun Campus).
Students can reserve study seats, borrow and return books in the library.
Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday 7:00-22:30

How to Reserve a Seat in the Library

  2. Click "Reserve Seat（座位预约）" > Select Date and Seat > Confirm Selection > Choose a seat > Book Now

Sunshine Service Team 阳光服务队

The Sunshine Service Team helps students at the Liangxiang Campus with lost and found notices.
Sunshine Service Team Office: South Campus, East side of the Community Cultural Square under the bridge
Office Hours: Monday to Friday 10:00-19:00

PS【Lost and Found Locations】:

  • North Cafeteria: Next to the manager's office on the first floor, near the corridor on the third floor next to the east window
  • South Cafeteria: First floor water bar, third floor cashier
  • Halal Cafeteria: Left-hand side near the entrance
  • Library: First-floor service desk
  • Lijiao Building: Duty room next to room 103
  • Zongjiao Building: Duty room opposite ZongA101
  • Printing Shop: Ask the staff directly
  • East Cafeteria: Service desk next to the north entrance on the first floor, service desk next to the leftover tray area on the third floor
  • Wencui Building: Lost and found location to be determined, inquire at the duty room on the second floor of Wen2 or Wen3

About Dormitories

Curfew: 6:00-24:00
Rules:

  1. Do not leave clutter in the hallways
  2. No smoking inside the dormitory
  3. Do not disturb others' rest

About B1

The B1 Building for international students has a few classrooms. If no class is being held, the rooms can be used for self-study.
Rules:

  1. Do not litter
  2. Do not leave your belongings in B1
  3. No eating inside the B1 classrooms

About the Laundry Room

How to use the washing machines/dryers:

  1. Choose a washing machine to use
  2. Place your dirty clothes inside (make sure there are no tissues)
  3. Add laundry detergent or disinfectant
  4. Scan the QR code of the washing machine, select the laundry service, and make the payment
  5. Remember the washing time and be on time to pick up your clothes

  2. When does winter break start?
    After the exam week, you can check the academic calendar (generally, the break starts right after exams).
